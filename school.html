Founded in 1957 as Saint Columban School by Fr. Sean Nolan of the Missionary Society of Saint Columban, with help from the Sisters of Saint Paul de Chartres. It has grown to be the largest Diocesan School of Pagadian.

In 1978, ownership was transferred to the Diocese of Pagadian, ushering in a new era of academic excellence and spiritual growth under Filipino leadership.
